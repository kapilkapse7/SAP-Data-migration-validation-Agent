SAP Master Data Management (MDM) — Functional Specification
Material Master Preload Validation Rules
Document Version: 1.0
================================================================================

This document defines field-level validation rules for SAP Material Master
preload data during the data migration cutover phase.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 1: BASIC DATA (MARA)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Field: MATNR
Rule: Cannot be blank

Field: MTART
Rule: Must equal FERT

Field: MEINS
Rule: Must be one of: EA, KG, L, M

Field: MATKL
Rule: Cannot be blank

Field: BRGEW
Rule: Must be numeric

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 2: SALES ORGANIZATION DATA (MVKE)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Field: VKORG
Rule: Cannot be blank

Field: VTWEG
Rule: Must equal 10

Field: DWERK
Rule: Cannot be blank

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 3: PLANT DATA (MARC)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Field: WERKS
Rule: Must be one of: 1000, 2000, 3000

Field: DISMM
Rule: Must equal PD

Field: BESKZ
Rule: Must be one of: E, F

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 4: ADDITIONAL CONSTRAINTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Field: MAKTX
Rule: Maximum length 40

Field: EAN11
Rule: Must match pattern ^[0-9]{13}$

Field: ERSDA
Rule: Must be in YYYY-MM-DD format

================================================================================
END OF DOCUMENT
